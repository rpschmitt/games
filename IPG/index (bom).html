Ideabob's Prototype Gallery

Welcome! I'm Ideabob, an amateur game developer. In the beginning of 2019 I started building games with this nifty tool called Construct 3 and I loved it. The result was a lot of abandoned prototypes and some half-finished games I still work on whenever I can. The Prototype Gallery is meant to share these games with the world, free of charge. I intend to add more content regularly, so don't forget to check back. To play them, click the buttons to browse then click the image to open the game in a new window (make sure your browser supports HTML5). I hope you enjoy your stay, have fun!
Don't hesitate to contact me at ideabob@mail.com!
